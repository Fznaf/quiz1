SOTO BETAWI.

Soto Betawi is a beef soup dish that is well-known in Jakarta, Indonesia. The soup is made with a creamy coconut milk broth and seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices, giving it a savory and aromatic taste. It is typically served with rice cakes, potatoes, and crispy shallots for added texture and flavor.

GADO-GADO.

Gado-gado is a popular salad dish in Jakarta, Indonesia, made with boiled vegetables such as spinach, cabbage, and bean sprouts, along with tofu, tempeh, and hard-boiled eggs. The salad is then dressed with a rich and creamy peanut sauce, adding a nutty and slightly sweet flavor to the dish. It is often served as a healthy and satisfying meal option in Jakarta.
