THIS is JAKARTA.

Jakarta is the capital city of Indonesia, located on the northwest coast of the island of Java. With a population of over 10 million people, Jakarta is the largest city in Indonesia and one of the most populous cities in the world. The city is a hub for business, politics, and culture, and is home to many international corporations, government institutions, and historic landmarks. Jakarta is a city of contrasts, with modern skyscrapers towering over traditional markets and historic neighborhoods. The city's diverse population includes people from all over Indonesia and the world, resulting in a rich blend of cultures and traditions. Jakarta is also known for its vibrant nightlife, with countless restaurants, bars, and clubs catering to all tastes and styles.
