MONAS.

Monas, located in Jakarta, is a towering monument that symbolizes Indonesia's struggle for independence and is one of the most iconic landmarks in the city.

KOTA TUA.

Kota Tua is the historic heart of Jakarta, Indonesia, that was the center of Dutch colonial government during the 17th to the 19th century. The district features well-preserved colonial-era buildings and landmarks, such as the Fatahillah Museum, the Old City Hall, and the Jakarta Kota railway station. Today, Kota Tua is a popular tourist destination and a cultural hub, where visitors can explore the historical significance and heritage of Jakarta.
